About the app

Built for people who are tired of "just delete the app" advice

Most digital wellness tools either shame you into quitting cold turkey or gamify your behavior with streaks that feel like another obligation. Im Not Lazy takes a different path: understand your habits, reduce friction gradually, track how you feel, and build a personalized plan based on what you actually want back — study time, fitness, sleep, creative work, or real social connection.

Calm, not punitive

No red alerts, no "you failed" messages. The app supports small wins and honest self-reporting without judgment.

Evidence-informed, not preachy

The Learn feed explains dopamine loops, algorithm design, and attention economics in plain language — so you understand why scrolling hooks you.

Your data, your control

We collect only what's needed for the app to work. GDPR/CCPA-friendly deletion flow removes your account and all data in Settings.

Full functionality

Every screen, built for real daily use

Five core experiences plus settings and legal pages — all wired to Firebase for persistence across devices.

1. Onboarding Quiz

Your journey starts with an honest conversation — not a lecture. The onboarding flow confirms you're 18+, creates your account, and asks three key questions:

  • Which apps waste your time (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and more)
  • How many hours per day you estimate scrolling
  • What you'd do with reclaimed time (study, fitness, sleep, creativity)

From your answers, the app generates a personalized detox path using deterministic local logic — no AI APIs, no black box. Recommendations like notification reduction, feed friction, time blocks, and evening cutoffs are tailored to your specific combination of apps and goals.

Everything saves to Firestore tied to your authenticated account.

2. Setup Wizard

Knowledge without action doesn't change habits. The Setup Wizard walks you through concrete, step-by-step hardening for each platform — starting with Instagram, with YouTube ready on the same JSON-driven pattern.

  • Each step has a title, instructions, optional tips, and warnings
  • Check off steps as you complete them — progress persists to Firestore
  • Platform tabs let you switch between Instagram and YouTube guides
  • Completing all steps earns a badge on your profile

Step content lives in a local JSON file, so new platforms can be added without rewriting the UI.

How it works

Four steps to reclaimed time

No extreme detox. Just a structured path that meets you where you are.

1

Take the quiz

Confirm you're 18+, pick your time-wasting apps, estimate scroll hours, and choose what you'd do with reclaimed time.

2

Get your path

Receive a personalized detox plan with prioritized recommendations — notification cuts, feed friction, time blocks, and more.

3

Run the wizard

Follow step-by-step setup guides for Instagram and YouTube. Check off each step as you complete it.

4

Check in daily

Track mood, scroll time, and intentions. Watch your streak grow and see patterns emerge over weeks.

Privacy & trust

Built with data minimization in mind

We don't sell your data. We don't track you across the web. We store only what the app needs to function.

What we store

Email, onboarding answers, wizard progress, daily check-ins, mood ratings, self-reported scroll hours, and streak stats. That's it.

What we don't store

No location, no contacts, no photos, no device fingerprinting, no third-party analytics, no ad tracking.

Your rights

Delete your account and all data anytime from Settings. GDPR and CCPA-friendly access, correction, and deletion flows.
